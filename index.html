Hey! I'm Artem. I'm participating a coursera startup engineering course made by two awesome guys from Stanford.

The idea of the course is to learn how to launch a startup quickly, not hiring designers and programmers - just spending several week working on the idea, investigating the market, coding a basic website, and ultimately making a first jump.

However I think that it is still not enough. The best of you need a stage to present your projects, to share your passion and to get more chances to bring your product real.

Startup Developer conference is a meeting point for the best courserians from the Startup Engineering course and also entrepreneurs, investors and media people.

There are 100,000 people attending this course, why not to give an extra bonus for the best ones -- let us help them to create the show and spin of their ideas. We will bring a microphone and give them a chance to present their projects out loud.
